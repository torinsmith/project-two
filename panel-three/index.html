All pies are rated according to these guidelines:

  • Each reviewed pie will recieve a score on the 10-point scale to the first decimal, with "10.0" being a perfect pie.
  • Pepperoni is a preferred topping, but is not required. Additional toppings are allowed on a case by case basis at the reviewer's discretion.
  • The following toppings are STRICTLY prohibited:
    • Pineapple

Rules are subject to change. Contact us to suggest rule changes.
