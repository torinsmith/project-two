Rules

Nado's Pizza Rankings Official Rules
  1. Each pizza is ranked on a 10-point scale to the first decimal.
  2. Every ranked pie must be pepperoni. Other acceptable toppings include:
    • Onion
    • Banana Pepper
    • Multiple Cheeses

Official Disclaimer

Torin Smith's Official Pizza Rankings is NOT affiliated with any of the restaurants or establishments that appear in the rankings. All rankings, comments and guest input are fairly reported and are based on the experience of the author.

Shall an owner/manager of a restaurant be unhappy or dissatisfied with their ranking, they are advised to simply make better pizza.

Requests for a review of a ranking will be evaluated at the discretion of the author, and thus are neither promised nor prohibited.
